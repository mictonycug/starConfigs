1. First you will need to set up the four rewasd slots, go to settings in the top right of rewasd and click slots. 2. From here click on each slot and enable it. Next click on apply for the config you want to use then unlock your mouse and press on the second slot in the bottom right (it's a circle). 4. Repeat this for each config (heavy and light, if you applied medium first). 5. Keep the forth slot empty as this will be used to disable rewasd.
